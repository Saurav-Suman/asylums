1What is the Date & Venue for the Summer Bootcamp 2019?

2 What is the process for the registration of the Bootcamp?

Step 1: Select the course according to the pre-requisites
Step 2: Register yourself by filling a small application form
Step 3: An interview will be scheduled accordingly
Step 4: Can permanently register yourself by paying registration amount (if got selected in the interview)
Step 5: Can try and apply for another course or can even try in next session (if got rejected in the interview)
